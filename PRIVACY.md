# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: March 16, 2026**

## Overview

LinkedIn Skills Bulk Delete is a browser extension that respects your privacy. We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information or usage data.

## Data Collection

**LinkedIn Skills Bulk Delete collects ZERO data.**

Specifically:
- No browsing history
- No personal information
- No cookies or local storage
- No analytics or telemetry
- No tracking pixels
- No user identifiers
- No network requests to external servers

## How the Extension Works

The extension operates entirely locally in your browser:

1. **Detection**: Checks if the current page is a LinkedIn skills detail page
2. **UI Injection**: Adds checkboxes and a toolbar for bulk skill selection
3. **Deletion**: Automates LinkedIn's native delete flow for each selected skill
4. **That's it**: No data leaves your browser

## Permissions

The extension requires minimal permissions:

- **Content Scripts on LinkedIn.com**: Required to detect the skills page and inject the bulk-delete UI
- **No additional permissions**: The extension does not request access to:
  - Your browsing history
  - Your personal data
  - Other websites
  - Network requests

## Third-Party Services

LinkedIn Skills Bulk Delete does **not** use any third-party services, including:
- No analytics providers (Google Analytics, etc.)
- No crash reporting services
- No advertising networks
- No content delivery networks
